The Last Of Cat
Cat Hero is Earth's most beloved warrior, having battled through many valiant victories, he is Earth's last hope when an alien race decides to attack Earth. As the courageous Cat Hero, you will have to battle through the enemy and danger to save the Earth.
Flappy Cat
The evil aliens are attacking Earth, Cat Hero must pass obstacles to reach his legendary spaceship to fight back.
Tutorial: left mouse clicks to use the jetpack, try not to touch the ground or the obstacles
Catsteroid-2
Knowing that he can’t withstand the enemy attacks, he heads for the enemy mothership in the hope of being able to destroy the enemy headquarters to stop the attack in its tracks.
Tutorial: W A S D to move, Spacebar or Left Mouse to Fire, avoid asteroid and enemy fire
Space Dungeon
After successfully infiltrating the enemy mother ship, Cat Hero has to fight through enemy waves to try and reach the command room to disable the attack.
Tutorial: W A S D to Move, Mouse to Aim, Left mouse to Fire. Avoid enemy fire and trap.
Planet Defense
Unfortunately, our hero was unable to stop the attack in its track, under heavy firepower from the enemy, he ultimately must retreat to Earth to defend Earth to the last breath.
Tutorial: use the mouse to look around and aim, left click to fire. Defend Earth, don’t let any enemy asteroid touch Earth.
